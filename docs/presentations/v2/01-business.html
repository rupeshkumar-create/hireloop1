Aarya screening call (planned)

Once jobs + candidate + recruiter basics work: every candidate schedules a 15–20 min Deepgram voice call with Aarya.

Purpose 1 · Candidate

Richer profile → better jobs

Experience, career path, preferences from the call update the graph so matches get more relevant.

Purpose 2 · Recruiter

Screening on apply

When a candidate applies to a recruiter JD, backend screens call transcript + JD and shows a screening result to the recruiter.

Prerequisite: stable ingest/match and working candidate + recruiter MVP. Builds on Deepgram voice (S05 exists as a lighter session today).
